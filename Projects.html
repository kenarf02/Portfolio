holo knights td

Holo Knights TD is a game made during my internship in Apptimia. It was made in unity3D and MRTK for microsoft hololens2. It is a fully playable tower defense game with all available AR features as well as: placing and upgrading various towers, randomly generated maps and enemy spawns, status effects (slow, burn) and many more.

Boing boing ball

Boing boing ball is my most recent, soon-to-be-released mobile game. It is a one-tap, physics-based adventure through colorful, yet simple and elegant worlds. The player's task is to launch a ball through the level to reach the flag and collect the stars. It is my proudest attempt to make a mobile game, it taught me more about level design than programming, however, it was a good try to make something fun, playing with ideas and making up simple mechanics.

barry and the zombies

Barry and the zombies is a top-down roguelike game. The player finds himself in a base full of dangerous enemies, traps, and bosses. One's main goal is to explore and find out where did the zombies come from. In the game, one can find a multitude of items, such as weapons, usable items with a variety of different effects, and passive items, granting bonuses for the player. "Barry and the zombies" is my best game project so far, which taught me a lot when it comes to game design and programming. It has been played by a total of 50 players. The game was made entirely in Unity3D engine and programmed in C#. Every game experience is different because of procedural room generation as well as placement of the enemies and items. Download here. The code can be found on my github.
